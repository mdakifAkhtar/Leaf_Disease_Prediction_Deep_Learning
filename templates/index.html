About the Project

Bringing AI to the Farm

Why It Matters

Crop diseases cause significant yield losses worldwide. Early and accurate detection empowers farmers to act quickly, reducing crop loss and improving food security.

How It's Built

This application uses a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) trained on thousands of labeled leaf images to recognize visual patterns associated with 15 different disease and healthy classes.

Supported Crops

Currently supports Pepper Bell, Potato, and Tomato plants, covering common bacterial, fungal, viral, and pest-related conditions.
